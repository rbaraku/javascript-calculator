Make your masterpiece

When shopping for PC parts it is important to remember that not every part is compatible with each other. For example if you get an intel cpu, then you will need an intel chipset motherboard to go with it. To help make sure you don't run into any compatibility issues, refer to PcPartPicker for help on picking your parts. There you will be able to browse for parts and they provide compatibility notes on if your build has any issues or not. You can make a blueprint there and PcPartPicker also provides links to purchase your parts.
